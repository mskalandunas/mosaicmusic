Music & Sound

About

My name is Mike Skalandunas, and this is Mosaic- my boutique music and sound studio. I've been working as a freelance composer & sound designer since graduating from Berklee College of Music in 2011, with most of my work being for video games. I try to be as creative as possible by incorporating synthesis, sound design, live recording, and home-made instruments into my production.

Below, you'll be able to see more of what I have to offer, or you can check out my soundcloud and youtube channels.

Sound Design

Sounds created with passion and imagination for an indie world. From ethereal, to 8-bit, to realistic- I create each sound using foley, found objects, synthesis, and field recordings. If you'd like to hear examples of my sound design work, check out this youtube playlist. You'll find examples of games I've been a part of as well as audio redesigns.

Music

A soundtrack adds to the player's experience in ways that almost no other aspect of gameplay can. It has the power to create or break the immersion, and tug on the player's emotional sensibilities. I try my hardest to create dynamic soundtracks with both adaptive and linear pieces of music. I also do my best to prototype interactivity using tools created in Max, Pure Data, and JavaScript.
